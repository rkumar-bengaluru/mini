Description

Experience the racket with a World Champion flavor. The PVS Rackets are PV Sindhu's signature series racket with her initials marked on the Ultra Slim racket shaft.

This series boasts a unique combination of Aerotec beam system for faster swings and Dynamic Optimum frame for an enlarged sweet spot facilitating thunderous smashes.

This racket promises strength without compromising on the weight by using high-quality carbon graphite.

The Head heavy balance of the racket ensures smooth aggressive gameplay on the court. The high tensile and flexible shaft provides strength and stability to the chaotic nature of aggressive gameplay.
