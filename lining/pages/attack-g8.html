CUSHION

CUSHION is used in the main impact area of the midsole for providing cushioning. It is strategically placed to absorb shock and disperse pressure at the impact area and ensures smooth locomotion.

Non-Marking Rubber Outsole

You can bid adieu to all your fears of losing balance on court as this non-marking shoe comes equipped with an anti-slip rubber outsole that offers the perfect balance of friction and movement. The non-marking rubber outsole is of premium grade that is highly sturdy and durable making it an absolute value for money.

Energy Returning Midsole

This shoe warrants maximum energy return by absorbing shock helping you enjoy a smooth ride using the Energy Returning Mid technology. It offers the best possible performance, protection, and helps reduce the risk of injury significantly by ensuring complete protection.
