Description

The G-force Superlight Ignite 7 is a combination of lightweight, power, and flexibility. This head-heavy racket adds power to your smashes by maintaining a lightweight distribution. Engineered with a Dynamic Optimum Frame, this racket allows more consistent and higher hitting velocity, while the Aerotec Beam System reduces drag coefficient so you can enjoy faster swings all the way. To enhance the stability and durability of the racket by maintaining top-notch performance, the racket has also been incorporated with TB Nanotechnology.

DYNAMIC OPTIMUM FRAME X NANO POWER FRAME

This Speed Racket has been devised with both Dynamic Optimum Frame and Nano Power Frame Technology that maximizes the sweet spot for consistent and powerful strokeplay enabling you to go gung-ho on your opponent. All these are achieved while maximizing stability.

HIGH TENSILE SLIM SHAFT

The High Tensile Slim Shaft incorporates a unique elastic material which effectively balances the rebound and anti-torsion performance of the shaft. This allows the racket to enhance the power you put in your swing.

COMPRESSED GROOVE

The Ignite-7 is devised with Compressed Groove Technology. This allows you to make best use of it's Head Heavy Profile by reducing Air-Drag and enabling you to steer the racket effortlessly.
